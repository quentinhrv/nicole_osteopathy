ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Nicole and I've just arrived in Vancouver, where a new adventure awaits me as an osteopathic practitioner.

My choice to become an osteopathic practitioner stems from my desire to work in the field of health while favouring natural approaches. Osteopathy has enabled me to explore the subtleties of the human body, offering the possibility of healing holistically, with my own hands. My various internships, notably with athletes and handicapped children, have enriched my practice and enabled me to develop techniques adapted to each individual's needs.

As a member of Osteopathy BC, I am committed to maintaining high professional standards and following the latest advances in the discipline to offer my patients quality care.
